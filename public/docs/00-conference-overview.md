# Flink Forward Asia 2026 — Conference Overview

**Date**: June 26–27, 2026  
**Location**: Shenzhen, China  
**Organized by**: Alibaba Cloud  
**Created by**: Ververica  
**Diamond Partner**: NVIDIA  
**Theme**: 实时数据 智能未来 / Real-Time Data Powers the Future of AI

---

## Conference Venue

The conference was held in a large hotel/convention center in Shenzhen, featuring:

- **Main Hall (大宴会厅)**: Keynote sessions
- **Track Rooms (分论坛)**: Madrid 2, 3, 5, 6-1, 6-2 halls
- **VIP Room (VIP贵宾厅)**
- **Exhibition Area**: Alibaba Cloud, NVIDIA, and partner booths

![Venue Signage](/images/Weixin%20Image_20260627190222_5_25.jpg)

---

## Conference Theme

The central theme of Flink Forward Asia 2026 is **"Real-Time Data Powers the Future of AI"**, emphasizing the intersection of Apache Flink's real-time data processing capabilities with the emerging AI Agent era.
